The Breez hub connects the dots

Breez is a Lightning Network hub that connects individuals, merchants and Bitcoin exchanges to facilitate instantaneous bitcoin commerce.

When an individual or a merchant installs the Breez app, a payment channel to the Breez hub is automatically created. This means all entities (or nodes) connected to the Breez hub can easily transact using Lightning Network.

Since the Breez hub can also connect to other Lightning Networks nodes, transactions are not limited to Breez entities, but are spread across the entire network. Breez also partners with Bitcoin exchanges to provide fiat conversion services to its users and businesses.
